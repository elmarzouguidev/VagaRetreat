VagaRetreat Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

At VagaRetreat, we place paramount importance on the protection of your personal data. This policy details how we collect, use, and secure your information when booking your retreats and stays.

1. Data Collected
  • Profile Information: Last name, first name, email address, phone number, and travel preferences necessary for personalizing your experience.
  • Booking Details: History of viewed retreats, bookings made, and interactions with our partner instructors.
  • Technical Data: IP address, device type, and cookies to optimize your navigation on our platform.
  • Payment Data (Stripe): Your transactions are processed securely by Stripe. VagaRetreat does not store any full credit card numbers.
2. Use of Data
  • Ensuring the management and confirmation of your retreat bookings.
  • Improving our services and offering you adventures tailored to your desires.
  • Guaranteeing the security of transactions and preventing any fraudulent activity.
  • Communicating with you regarding your stays and VagaRetreat news.
3. Data Sharing
  • Your data is only transmitted to essential third parties: instructors for the organization of the retreat, and Stripe for payment.
  • VagaRetreat commits to never selling or renting your personal data to third parties for commercial purposes.
4. Security
  • We use SSL/TLS encryption protocols for all communications and transactions.
  • Access to your data is strictly limited to authorized personnel for processing your requests.
5. Your Rights
  • You have the right to access, rectify, and delete your data. For any request, contact us at: support@vaga-retreat.com.

By using VagaRetreat, you accept the terms of this privacy policy.
