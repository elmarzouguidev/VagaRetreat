About VagaRetreat

VagaRetreat grows from a shared passion for conscious travel, holistic health, and cultural exchange. You travel to slow down, reflect, and build real connections. Each stay supports personal growth grounded in real experience.

Our vision

Modern life links screens and separates people. You reconnect through direct experience. Our retreats invite you to step away from routines and reconnect with yourself, the group, and nature. Each location supports focus, presence, and inner balance.

What we offer
  • Focused adventures: Atlas Mountain treks, minimalist surf retreats, immersive stays.
  • Qualified experts: Yoga, meditation, surf, and mountain guidance led by certified professionals.
  • Local impact: Partnerships with local communities for meaningful and lasting value.
Our team

The team operates from Marrakech. It brings together committed, multicultural profiles. You benefit from smooth organization, from location selection to the meals served. Every detail supports your experience.
